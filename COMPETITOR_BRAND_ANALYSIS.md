## Key Insights

### 1. Long Tail is Huge
Competitors don't just have major retailers - they have hundreds of small/emerging DTC brands that cater to niche audiences.

### 2. International Matters
Korean brands (StyleNanda, Chuu, etc.), Australian brands (Peppermayo, Showpo), European brands (Ganni, Mango) are essential for differentiation.

### 3. Multi-Category Coverage
Successful platforms don't just do clothing - they cover:
- Shoes (essential)
- Bags (high-value transactions)
- Jewelry (high engagement, affordable)
- Activewear (massive category)
- Swimwear (seasonal but important)

### 4. DTC Brands Drive Differentiation
- Everyone has Nordstrom and Zara
- Winners have: Ganni, Staud, Realisation Par, Mejuri
- These brands have better margins and affiliate terms
