Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 3, 2026

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Welcome to Muse ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Your Privacy Matters: We only collect information necessary to provide you with personalized fashion discovery. You have full control over your data and can delete your account at any time.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, name, username, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Shopping preferences, favorite brands, style preferences, and size information
  • Optional Information: Location (for nearby store recommendations), age, gender

1.2 Information from Third-Party Services (With Your Consent)

  • Instagram Data: When you connect your Instagram account, we collect:
    • List of accounts you follow (to identify fashion influencers)
    • Basic profile information (username, profile picture)
    • Public content for style analysis
  • Gmail Data: When you connect your Gmail account, we collect:
    • Order confirmation emails from retailers
    • Purchase history and product information
    • Shipping and order details

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent on pages, search queries
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
  • Analytics Data: App interactions, cart activity, conversion events
  • Session Data: Session duration, bounce rate, navigation patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide Our Services

  • Personalize your fashion discovery feed based on your preferences
  • Analyze your Instagram follows to understand your style
  • Extract purchase history from Gmail to recommend similar items
  • Show you products from brands and influencers you love
  • Provide shopping cart and checkout functionality
  • Generate affiliate links to retailers

2.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
  • Conduct A/B testing to enhance user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Develop new features

2.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send promotional emails (with your consent, opt-out available)

2.4 For Security and Compliance

  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect user safety and security

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Affiliate Partners

When you click on product links and make purchases, we share minimal information with affiliate networks (Rakuten, Commission Junction) to track referrals and earn commissions. This typically includes:

  • Order confirmation (purchase occurred)
  • Order value (for commission calculation)
  • No personally identifiable information is shared

3.2 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our services:

  • Cloud Hosting: To store and process data securely
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve performance
  • Email Services: To send transactional emails

These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Protect user safety

3.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

We Never Sell Your Data: We do not and will not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with admin authentication
  • Input Validation: Protection against SQL injection and XSS attacks
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute force attacks
  • Audit Logging: All security-relevant actions are logged
  • Regular Testing: Automated security testing and monitoring

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format

5.2 Correction and Update

  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate data

5.3 Deletion

  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request deletion of specific information
  • Revoke third-party app permissions (Instagram, Gmail)

5.4 Opt-Out

  • Unsubscribe from promotional emails
  • Disable analytics tracking
  • Disconnect third-party services

5.5 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records)
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytics

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Instagram

When you connect your Instagram account:

  • You authorize us to access your Instagram follows and public content
  • We use this data solely for fashion style analysis
  • You can disconnect Instagram at any time from your account settings
  • Data is deleted when you disconnect or delete your account
  • We comply with Meta's Platform Terms and Instagram API Terms

6.2 Gmail

When you connect your Gmail account:

  • You authorize us to read order confirmation emails only
  • We scan for purchase history to recommend products
  • We do not read, store, or access any other emails
  • You can disconnect Gmail at any time from your account settings
  • Data is deleted when you disconnect or delete your account
  • We comply with Google API Services User Data Policy

6.3 Retailer Websites

When you click product links to retailer websites:

  • You leave our app and enter the retailer's website
  • The retailer's privacy policy applies
  • We are not responsible for retailer privacy practices
  • We receive referral information for commission purposes only

14. Data Deletion Instructions

To Delete Your Account and All Data:

  1. Open the Muse app
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

To Disconnect Third-Party Services:

  1. Open the Muse app
  2. Go to Settings → Connected Accounts
  3. Tap "Disconnect" next to Instagram or Gmail
  4. Confirm disconnection

All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
